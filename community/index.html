Youth Art Connect

Visit Website

Youth Art Connect is a not-for-profit community group, set up in 2021 to introduce the therapeutic benefits of art to young people. They provide regular youth and community groups across Cornwall as well as pop up events throughout the year.

Facing limitations in team size and resources, they sought assistance with their online events system and event organization. Ment*r Group stepped in to provide invaluable support by creating a brand-new website featuring a seamlessly integrated booking system, effectively streamlining the registration process. Recognizing the importance of accessibility for their target audience, the website was designed to be fully inclusive.

In addition to the website development, we have taken care of hosting and ongoing support, allowing Youth Art Connect to focus on running their events without worrying about technical complexities. Since its launch in January 2023, the new website has garnered immense satisfaction from the Youth Art Connect team, playing a pivotal role in their aspirations to evolve into a full-fledged charitable organization.

Mentor Group is not just a partner but a guiding light for those yearning for change. From the very beginning, they extended their hands wholeheartedly, understanding not only our needs but also the dreams and hopes we longed to realize. With unwavering sincerity, they crafted every detail with love and care, ensuring that each step taken would bring meaningful benefits to many. Their dedication goes beyond professionalism—it is a calling, a heartfelt mission to create something truly impactful. We are not just grateful; we are deeply moved and proud of Mentor Group’s extraordinary commitment to making the world a better place.
